How It Works

1

Create Your Listing

Fill out our simple form with details about your space, add photos, and set your price. You'll also need to include any special features or house rules.

2

Once your listing is live, guests can book your space. Provide them with check-in details and be available to answer any questions they might have.

3

Get Paid

You'll receive payment shortly after your guests check in. Our secure payment system ensures you get paid on time, every time.
